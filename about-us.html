When it comes to having an engine that performs well – and never lets you down – the devil is in the detail. You need to know that what is in your engine bay not only looks good quality but also offers workmanship and materials you can rely on 100%. Many times under race conditions, or on modern roads, only the best will do. And that is especially true of silicone hoses. They need to be strong enough to cope with whatever the race track or road puts them through. And whatever task they are there to achieving – boost, induction, coolant or breather hoses.
